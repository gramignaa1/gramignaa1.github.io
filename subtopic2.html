Benefits of Assistive Technology in Education

Resource 1

URL PubMed Article
AuthorAlnahdi et al.
Abstract This study examines the positive impact of assistive technology on students with disabilities in educational settings. The authors report that AT improves academic performance, independence, and student engagement. Benefits include increased access to curriculum materials, enhanced communication, and greater participation in classroom activities. The article also highlights how assistive technology supports inclusive education by allowing students with disabilities to learn alongside their peers. The findings suggest that when teachers receive adequate training and support, assistive technology becomes a powerful tool for improving learning outcomes and promoting equity in education.

Resource 2

URL Taylor & Francis Article
AuthorR. Borgestig et al.
Abstract This article explores how assistive technology supports learning and participation for students with disabilities. The authors describe improvements in academic skills, motivation, and self-confidence when AT is integrated effectively. They also discuss how technology fosters independence by reducing reliance on adults. The study emphasizes the importance of collaboration between teachers, students, and support staff to maximize benefits. Overall, the research demonstrates that assistive technology plays a critical role in creating inclusive and accessible educational environments.
